‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my Apple Watch with iPhone?

1. Make sure both devices are on and nearby

2. Open "Golden Point" app on your Apple Watch

3. Open the app on your iPhone and go to "Home" tab

4. Tap "Register Apple Watch" and follow instructions

5. Once connected, all matches will sync automatically

How do I configure match rules?

1. Go to "Settings" on your iPhone

2. Select "Match Configuration"

3. Adjust options according to your preferences:

  • Golden Point (enabled/disabled)
  • Advantages before Golden Point
  • Tie Break
  • Points per Set

4. Changes will automatically apply to your Apple Watch

How do I start a match on Apple Watch?

1. Open the app on your Apple Watch

2. Tap "New Match"

3. Select your team (A or B)

4. Start scoring points with + and - buttons

5. The match will be saved automatically when finished

Can I use the app without Apple Watch?

Yes, you can use Golden Point with iPhone only to view statistics and configure settings. However, for real-time match tracking, you need a compatible Apple Watch.

How do I enable training tracking?

1. Go to "Settings" > "Match Configuration"

2. Enable "Training Tracking"

3. Grant HealthKit permissions when requested

4. Workouts will be automatically recorded in the Health app
