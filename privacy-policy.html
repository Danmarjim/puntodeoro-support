Privacy Policy

Punto de Oro - Padel Tracking App
Last Updated: December 2024
Important: This privacy policy explains how Punto de Oro ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our padel tracking application available on the App Store.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health and Fitness Data

With your explicit consent, Punto de Oro integrates with Apple HealthKit to collect and process:

  • Workout Data: Exercise sessions, duration, and activity type
  • Health Metrics: Heart rate during workouts (if available)
  • Activity Data: Steps taken, active energy burned, distance covered
  • Workout Statistics: Training frequency and performance metrics

1.2 Match and Performance Data

We collect information about your padel matches and performance, including:

  • Match Statistics: Scores, points won/lost, service efficiency
  • Game Configuration: Match settings, rules preferences
  • Performance Metrics: Break points, golden points, match duration
  • Historical Data: Past match records and improvement tracking

1.3 Device and Technical Data

  • Device Information: iPhone and Apple Watch synchronization data
  • App Usage: Features used, preferences, and app interaction data
  • Technical Data: App version, device model, iOS version

1.4 Subscription and Payment Data

For Premium features, we process payment information through:

  • Apple App Store: Payment processing and subscription management
  • RevenueCat: Subscription status and entitlement management

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Track and analyze your padel match performance
  • Synchronize data between your iPhone and Apple Watch
  • Provide personalized statistics and insights
  • Generate match reports and performance trends

2.2 Health Integration

  • Record workouts in Apple Health with your consent
  • Display health metrics related to your padel training
  • Provide comprehensive fitness tracking

2.3 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • UserDefaults: App preferences and settings
  • Local Database: Match history and statistics
  • HealthKit: Health data (managed by Apple)

3.3 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure API communications
  • Regular security updates
  • Compliance with Apple's security standards

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

  • When you explicitly authorize data sharing
  • Health data shared with Apple Health (with your permission)

4.2 Service Providers

  • Payment processing through Apple App Store
  • Subscription management through RevenueCat

4.3 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • To prevent fraud or abuse
